CrosumMed

CrosumMed is an AI-powered pharmaceutical ecosystem portal that transforms complex drug data into clear, actionable knowledge. We aggregate medicine information, research articles, and pharmaceutical news from trusted government databases and scientific sources — making it accessible to patients, students, researchers, and healthcare professionals across India.

What We Offer

Three pillars of pharmaceutical intelligence

Search Any Pharma Product

Find any pharmaceutical product across 10 categories — oral medicines, topical treatments, respiratory devices, injectables, AYUSH products, wound care, medical devices, baby care, wellness supplements, and more. Our intelligent search understands partial names, generics, and even misspellings.

Every product page provides dosage information, side effects, chemical composition, pharmacological history, and direct buy links to trusted Indian pharmacy platforms so you can compare prices without opening multiple tabs.

Research Articles & Clinical Data

Access biomedical research articles sourced from PubMed, ClinicalTrials.gov, MedlinePlus, and other NIH databases. Browse across 8 scientific categories including clinical trials, drug pharmacology, disease treatment guides, drug safety alerts, and regulatory updates.

Filter by publication year, category, and keywords. Every article card shows the title, source institution, publication date, status, and a context-matched preview — so you can evaluate relevance before diving in.

Pharmaceutical Industry News

Stay updated with the latest developments in the pharmaceutical industry. From drug approvals and clinical trial results to company earnings and policy changes, CrosumMed aggregates news from WHO, FDA, CDSCO, and leading industry publications.

Sort by newest or oldest, filter by year range, and search across headlines and article content. Each news card includes source attribution and direct links to the original publication.
