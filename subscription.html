You may cancel your subscription at any time from your Profile page. Upon cancellation:

  • Your premium benefits will continue until the end of your current billing period.
  • No further charges will be made after cancellation.
  • You will revert to the free tier once your current billing period expires.

Non-Refundable Policy

All subscription payments are non-refundable. Once a payment has been successfully processed, it cannot be reversed, refunded, or credited to future billing cycles. This includes:

  • Monthly subscription charges that have already been processed
  • Partial month charges (we do not prorate refunds)
  • Charges for months where you did not use the platform

By subscribing, you acknowledge and accept this non-refundable policy. We recommend using the free tier of CrosumMed to evaluate the platform before subscribing.

Payment Security

All payments are processed through Razorpay, which is:

  • PCI-DSS Level 1 certified (highest level of payment security compliance)
